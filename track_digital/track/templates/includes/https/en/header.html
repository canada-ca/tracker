How do I read this table?

Canadians rely on the Government of Canada to provide secure digital services and expect government websites to be secure and private. The Government of Canada is committed to ensuring that all publicly accessible government websites and services are configured to provide service through a secure connection, as outlined in the IM/IT Strategic Plan 2017-21, and the upcoming Security Policy Implementation Notice (SPIN).

This dashboard measures how well federal government domains are meeting best practices on the web. Implementing the HTTPS protocol (https://), along with approved encryption algorithms, offers a level of security and privacy that users should expect from Government of Canada web services.
